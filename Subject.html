Kinetic Design

Kinetic design in digital design is designing all kinds of kinetic visual effects for user interfaces. The visual effects mean various animations and transitions. A drop-down menu animation and a button color transitions on hover are examples of such effects.But, designing visual effects is always harder than expected. It takes more time than it looks. Even one small animation requires a lot of decision makings behind the scene. Visual effects are easier to request but harder to create. The problem is that designers and developers are usually the only ones who aware of them.
